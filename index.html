Weight Conscious Tracker

Weight Conscious Tracker is designed to assist individuals, friends, families or weight watching groups in tracking individual progresses as they challenge themselves and/or others. Profiles for an adult and child will be entered in the appropiate form. The information will be stored under the person's name. The APP will maintain the data for height, age and weight. Age and weight will update with new entries for both; however, height for the adult will remain the same.

Adult

The Weight Conscious Tracker feature for the adult will store their information in a database and display changes to their age, and weight as they continue to participate.




Scroll down to search if the person is in the database










Child

The Weight Conscious Tracker feature for the child will store their information in a database and display changes to their hieght, age, and weight as they grow.
